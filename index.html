Your Implant Journey — Step by Step
1
Free Consultation
We assess your teeth, take X-rays, and explain the full process and cost with no obligation.
2
Implant Placement
A titanium post is gently placed in your jawbone under local anaesthesia — most patients feel very little discomfort.
3
Healing Period
The implant integrates with your bone over a few weeks. We'll monitor your progress carefully.
4
Crown Fitting
Your custom crown is fixed on top — perfectly colour-matched and shaped to complete your smile.
